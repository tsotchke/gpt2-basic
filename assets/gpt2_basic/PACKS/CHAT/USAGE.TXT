CHAT pack

Purpose:
  Use this pack for ordinary conversation. It is the default pack in the
  interactive QEMU demo because it is meant for talking, not a specific
  DOS or office task.

How it works:
  The pack uses its local PACKS\CHAT\MODEL checkpoint and retrieves short
  chat notes from HELP.TXT when a topic matches. It is also trained from
  GOLDEN.TXT common English dialogue and LEXICON.TSV grammar entries, so
  ordinary short prompts do not depend on a lookup hit. The current model
  uses a 4096-token lexicon vocabulary. The answers are deliberately brief so
  the DOS console can show Thinking token progress, stream Answer pieces as
  they are generated, and return to the prompt quickly.

How to use it:
  Just type a sentence at the > prompt. Use /about to see this help again.
  Use /u and /d to page through the transcript, or /h to show history.

Good prompts:
  Hello, what can you do?
  I am bored.
  Tell me a joke.
  Do you like music?
  I want to talk about this DOS demo.
  Give me one idea for improving the demo.
  What are your limits?

Actions:
  chat, ask, idea, explain, cancel
